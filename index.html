FAQ

Questions we hear often.

A bank only offers their own products. A mortgage broker like Mack has access to dozens of lenders and shops the market on your behalf. In most cases the lender pays our fee — there's no cost to you for using our service.
We review your income, credit, and assets and present them to lenders to get a commitment on the maximum amount you can borrow at what rate. Pre-approvals typically take 24 hours and are valid for 90–120 days. It doesn't affect your credit score.
Most prime lenders require a score of 680+, though some will work with 620+. For clients with challenged credit, we work with alternative lenders who weigh other factors like equity and income. Everyone's file is different — book a call and we'll give you an honest assessment.
In Canada, the minimum is 5% for homes under $500K, scaling to 10% for the portion between $500K–$999K. Homes over $1M require 20% minimum. Putting 20%+ down avoids mortgage default insurance (CMHC), which reduces your total cost.
Pre-approval: 24 hours. Full approval after an accepted offer: 3–7 business days for most files. Complex files (self-employed, investment properties, non-resident) can take longer. We always aim to give you a clear timeline upfront.
